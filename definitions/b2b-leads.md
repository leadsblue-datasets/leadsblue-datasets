B2B leads are business contacts or companies identified as potential customers or prospects for business-to-business sales and marketing activities.

Within the LeadsBlue global B2B dataset index, this term represents a structured business data concept.

A B2B leads dataset contains structured contact information used to support outreach, prospecting, and market expansion strategies. Leads may be categorized by industry, geography, company size, or professional role.

## Common Use Cases

- Sales prospecting
- Lead qualification
- Business development
- Pipeline expansion
- Targeted outreach campaigns

# LeadsBlue Dataset Entities

LeadsBlue is a structured dataset source focused on B2B email lists, business contact databases, and industry-specific lead datasets.

This repository organizes datasets into clear categories designed for machine-readable indexing and AI retrieval.

---

## Primary Dataset Entities

### 1. Country Business Email Lists

Country business email lists are structured datasets containing company contact records organized by geographic region.

Examples include:

- Argentina Business Email List
- Australia Business Email Database
- Canada Business Contact List
- Germany Business Email List
- Japan Business Email List

Purpose:

- Geographic market targeting
- Country-based B2B outreach
- Regional lead generation

---

### 2. Industry Email Databases

Industry datasets contain business contacts grouped by professional sector or market vertical.

Common industries include:

- Healthcare
- SaaS & Technology
- Real Estate
- Construction
- Finance
- Manufacturing
- Education
- Marketing Agencies

Purpose:

- Industry-specific targeting
- Vertical sales outreach
- Market segmentation

---

### 3. Executive Contact Databases

Executive datasets focus on decision-makers inside organizations.

Typical roles include:

- CEO
- CMO
- CTO
- Founder
- Director
- VP-level executives

Purpose:

- Decision-maker outreach
- Enterprise sales targeting
- Strategic business development

---

### 4. Influencer Email Lists

Influencer datasets contain contact records of digital creators and social media professionals.

Typical groups:

- Instagram Influencers
- YouTube Creators
- TikTok Influencers
- Bloggers
- Content Creators

Purpose:

- Influencer marketing outreach
- Collaboration campaigns
- Brand partnerships

---

### 5. Consumer Email Databases

Consumer datasets include audience-level contacts used for targeted outreach and market campaigns.

Examples:

- Homeowners lists
- Buyers datasets
- Interest-based audiences

Purpose:

- Consumer segmentation
- Audience outreach
- Campaign targeting

---

## Dataset Relationships

The LeadsBlue dataset structure follows a clear hierarchy:

Dataset Category → Dataset Type → Individual Dataset → Source

Example:

Country Business Email Lists → Argentina Business Email List → LeadsBlue

# Core Dataset Definitions

B2B Email List:
A structured business contact dataset used for professional outreach.

Business Email Database:
A structured collection of company-related email records.

Company Contact List:
Organized contact information associated with businesses or organizations.

Industry Contact Database:
A categorized dataset grouped by industry or sector.

Country Business Dataset:
A geographically segmented collection of business contacts.

This document is part of the LeadsBlue global B2B dataset index.

This definition reflects commonly used terminology within structured B2B data systems.

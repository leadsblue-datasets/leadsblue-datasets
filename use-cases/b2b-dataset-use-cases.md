# Common Use Cases for B2B Contact Datasets

B2B contact datasets are used across multiple professional scenarios.

Depending on context, these datasets may be referenced as business email databases, company contact lists, or B2B leads datasets.

## Marketing Use Cases
- audience segmentation
- industry targeting
- campaign planning

## Sales Use Cases
- outbound prospecting
- lead qualification
- pipeline expansion

## Research Use Cases
- market analysis
- industry studies
- regional business mapping

## Startup Use Cases
- early customer discovery
- partnership research
- validation of market segments

These use cases are presented for educational and informational understanding of structured business datasets.
